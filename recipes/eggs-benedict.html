Eggs Benedict

Description

Eggs Benedict is a brunch specialty consisting of hot buttered English muffins, Canadian-style bacon, and poached eggs topped with a rich homemade Hollandaise sauce.

Ingredients

For the Hollandaise:

  • Egg yolks
  • Lemon juice
  • Water
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • White pepper
  • Butter
  • Salt

For the eggs Benedict:

  • Distilled white vinegar
  • Eggs
  • Ham or Bacon
  • English muffins
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Make the Hollandaise sauce.
  2. Poach the eggs.
  3. Brown the ham or bacon and toast the English muffins
  4. Assemble the eggs Benedict.
